Please note:
<br>
An additional CPU overclocking frequency (2,57 GHz) has been added !
<br>
This means, one time after kernel flash you need to correct your settings as follows:
<br>
<br>
if you use custom voltages (undervolting etc.), you need to switch to "No undervolting" profile and then back to what you had before. 
<br>
<br>
If you use presets, you need to switch to standard preset and then back to what you had before. Please note, preset have been modified to include the new OC frequency.
